# Executive Dashboard: Investment & Budget Performance (2025 Case Study)

## Project Overview

This project presents an executive-level Power BI dashboard designed to monitor investment allocation, budget execution, and actual spending performance.

The dashboard was developed as a case study based on a corporate budgeting scenario using anonymized and simulated data.

The goal of the dashboard is to provide management with clear insights into how investment budgets are allocated and executed across different categories and business units.

---

## Business Problem

Organizations need visibility into how effectively their investment budgets are utilized. Without proper monitoring, companies may face issues such as:

- Low budget absorption
- Delayed project execution
- Uneven investment allocation
- Lack of transparency across business units

This dashboard helps management track investment allocation, actual spending, and execution rates in a centralized view.

---

## Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes several key analytical views:

- Investment Allocation by Category
- Budget vs Actual Investment Comparison
- Monthly Budget vs Actual Analysis
- Budget Execution by Business Unit
- Program-level Budget Breakdown
- Dynamic filtering by category, month, and business unit

---

## Key Insights

Based on the analysis presented in the dashboard:

- The current budget execution rate is **15.05%**, indicating that a significant portion of the budget has not yet been utilized.
- **Investment Category Y** represents the majority of the allocated investment budget.
- Spending activity increases during mid-year and late-year periods.
- Several programs have allocated budgets but no actual spending, suggesting potential delays in project execution.
